ABOUT US

Your filters. Your match.

VISION

Our vision is to create a dating experience where people connect through personality, interests, and shared values before judging each other by appearance. In a world of endless swiping and perfect selfies, we want to slow things down and make online dating feel a little more human. By focusing on compatibility and curiosity, we hope to create a platform where meaningful connections can grow naturally.

Our mission is to build a dating platform where users discover potential matches through information, interests, and lifestyle instead of just photos. With Dating by Data, users can explore profiles and filter potential matches based on details such as location, education, and work. This allows people to focus on who someone is, rather than only how they look.

MISSION

VALUES

We believe that great connections start with personality and shared interests. Our platform is built around the idea that dating should feel authentic, respectful, and a little less superficial. We value curiosity, honest communication, and giving users control over how they discover potential matches. By focusing on meaningful information rather than only appearance, we want to encourage deeper and more genuine connections between people.

Our goal is to offer an alternative to traditional swipe-based dating apps. Instead of quick decisions based on photos, our platform encourages users to explore profiles through filtering and meaningful information. By helping users discover people with similar lifestyles and interests, we aim to create a dating experience that feels more thoughtful, engaging, and hopefully leads to better matches.
